ABOUT US

Welcome to Tea Time Cafe, your cozy haven in Arlington, Texas, where quality meets wellness in every cup! We take pride in curating a delightful selection of premium teas, artisanal coffees, and refreshing cold-pressed juices, all crafted with your health in mind. At Tea Time Cafe, we believe in the power of nature's best ingredients. Our teas are sourced from renowned growers, ensuring that every sip delivers rich flavors and numerous health benefits—from soothing chamomile to invigorating green tea. Each brew is not just a drink; it's a step toward better wellness. Our coffee is equally exceptional, sourced from ethical farms and roasted to perfection. Whether you prefer a robust espresso or a smooth cold brew, our coffee promises to awaken your senses while providing antioxidants that promote overall health. In addition to our exquisite beverages, our cold-pressed juices are made fresh daily, packed with nutrients that boost your immune system and enhance vitality. Each juice blends fruits and vegetables to create refreshing elixirs that nourish your body and rejuvenate your spirit. Join us at Tea Time Cafe, where every visit is a celebration of flavor and health. Discover how our quality teas, coffees, and juices can enrich your daily routine and elevate your well-being. We can’t wait to serve you!
